CONCEPT

Facing all of the above given issues in the college campus, we did a lot of research and ended up creating Local Loop. A hyperlocal delivery service made for the local region. The special thing about the project is you can spend as well as earn on the same application.

Order that bhel from your favorite street vendor; Get your package delivered to the post office; Get some help from the ones nearby with your projects.
Having some free time? Become a HERO and earn by helping others.

WHY US?

Because we are made to think different. Bringing the solution of the problem to the forefront with the motive to connect people living next to each other, we are inspired by the human relations.

In this modern world, when the humanity is connected and separated via the same device, we believe in the theory of relativity.

Having a team of likeminded people, makes it easier to relate our problems as well as formulate a realtime as well as sustainable solution that could be leveraged as a product that could benefit the humanity in more than one ways.
